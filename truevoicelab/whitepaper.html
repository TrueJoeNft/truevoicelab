The TrueVoice
Model

Each TrueVoice artist is a complete creative universe: original music, animated chapter series, live events, and a digital fan community. Every chapter of an artist's story ends as a song. Every song deepens the world.

How Each Artist Universe Is Born
1
THE IDEA
TrueVoice listens to the community and proposes a story concept and musical genre. What world does this artist inhabit? What conflict drives the narrative? The community validates before anything is built.
2
THE IMAGE
TrueVoice selects 3 existing NFT communities whose aesthetic aligns with the story and genre decided. TVOICE holders vote to choose which community will define the visual identity of the new artist. This gives dormant NFT communities new purpose — and gives TrueVoice a ready-made fanbase from day one.
3
THE CREATOR
TrueVoice opens a public call for story creators. Participants submit a brief script outline for Chapter 1. Selected creator receives 300–500 USDC + TVOICE tokens as reward. Since AI accelerates the creative process, this is an accessible opportunity for writers, storytellers and creators worldwide. The best story wins — not the biggest name.
4
THE HUMAN
Every AI artist has a human soul behind it. The community helps define what human skills the artist needs — voice, dance movement, charisma, a specific instrument. TrueVoice finds 3 candidates who embody those skills. Holders vote for who becomes the living reference behind the character. If the project demands it (for example, a dancer whose movement defines the character's animation), multiple human collaborators can be selected. The human brings authenticity. The AI brings scale.
5
AI PRODUCTION
TrueVoice enters full production mode. The format is always 5 chapters + 5 songs — each chapter ends as a song, each song deepens the story world. Music produced with AI tools (Suno, Udio). Animation produced with AI video pipelines (Kling, Runway). Human reference footage is used for lip sync and movement authenticity. The result is a complete entertainment universe at a fraction of traditional production cost.
6
THE LAUNCH
The artist enters the world. First single released as a gift — no token pitch, no NFT mention. Just the music and the story. The TFANS collection mint follows once organic traction is demonstrated. The community that helped build the artist now owns a piece of it.
The principle: We involve the community at least twice in every artist's creation — and we find more ways to include them with each new launch. A community that helped build the artist cares about its success in a way no marketing campaign can manufacture.

Go-to-Market
Strategy

TrueVoice does not launch as a crypto project. It launches as an entertainment project with a community ownership layer. The music and the story come first. The token opportunity comes after — for those who want to be part of what comes next.

The founding principle: We never ask anyone to bet blind. Each phase validates the previous one. Each success funds the next. If something doesn't work, we learn cheaply. If it works, we scale with confidence.
The Three-Phase Launch Funnel
01
MUSIC FIRST
The Gift — Summer Song
Ruffus's first single launches with minimal budget as a free gift to the world — no NFT pitch, no token mention. Just the music, the character, and Chapter 1 of the animated series. Distributed on Spotify, YouTube, TikTok. Targeted paid campaign to reggaetón-pop fans aged 16–25 in Spain and Latin America.

Purpose: Validate the artist before committing investor capital. If the song generates organic traction, it's proof the model works. If it doesn't, we learn cheaply and adjust before the mint.
02
STORY REVEAL
The Origin — Behind the Character
4–6 weeks after the song launch: reveal the making-of. Not the tech — the human story. How one person built this frame by frame in 2023 before the tools existed. The contrast between the old workflow (Blender, Midjourney, Mixamo, manual animation) and the new AI workflow is content in itself — and the kind of authentic storytelling that attracts press, crypto-native audiences, and potential investors.

Purpose: Convert music fans into project fans. Attract the Web3 audience that will become RUFANS holders.
03
OWNERSHIP
The Opportunity — Be Part of It
Only when organic traction is demonstrated: present the ownership opportunity. Whitelist opens for RUFANS Genesis. Early investors enter with small capital but with real creative power over the artist's development. The mint funds the Song 2 campaign with full AI animation and real marketing budget.

Purpose: Close the loop — fans who discovered the music can now own a piece of it.

How multipliers work: The total royalty pool (10% of consolidated net revenue from all artists) is distributed proportionally to staked tokens, weighted by tier multiplier. More tokens staked + higher tier = larger share of the pool. The total distributed never exceeds the pool — multipliers determine your share relative to other stakers, not an absolute percentage. During the first 12 months post-TGE, an additional monthly reward from the Community allocation (25% of supply) is distributed to all active stakers, ensuring early holders receive meaningful value while label revenue grows.
Governance model: TVOICE voting is consultative. The TrueVoice CEO retains final decision authority on all label operations. Projects that transfer full DAO control before product-market fit historically struggle with paralysis and poor decisions. As the label matures and proves its model, governance scope expands progressively — giving the community more power as the stakes become clearer.

Risk
Factors

Every project operates in a context of uncertainty. What separates serious projects from speculative ones is not the absence of risk — it's the quality of thinking applied to navigating it.

Risk Factor Context Our Response
Regulatory Evolution (MiCA) EU crypto regulation continues to evolve. MiCA may impose requirements that limit certain token activities in specific jurisdictions. TrueVoice OÜ is structured for EU compliance from day one. MiCA-specialist legal counsel is engaged before the TGE. If regulatory constraints in any jurisdiction limit growth, TrueVoice will evaluate relocating operations to a more favorable framework — the project is built to be portable.
NFT Market Sentiment NFT demand cycles are unpredictable. Speculative NFT markets have contracted significantly since 2021–2022. RUFANS are not speculative assets — they are fan memberships with real economic utility: royalty participation, event access, rental income, and exclusive content. The marketing strategy leads with the artist and the music, not the NFT. If "NFT" as a term creates friction, the product is reframed as a membership — the blockchain is infrastructure, not the pitch.
Founder-Led Early Stage Early-stage projects concentrated in a single founder carry execution dependency. This concentration is intentional and temporary. The founder has already demonstrated the ability to build the entire product stack alone — which is the strongest possible proof of conviction and capability. Capital raised is specifically allocated to scale the team and reduce this dependency. Vesting structures ensure long-term alignment throughout.
